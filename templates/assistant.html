Quick Scam Recognition Guide

🚩 Common Red Flags

  • Requests for upfront payment
  • Guaranteed returns or profits
  • Urgency and pressure tactics
  • Too good to be true offers
  • Requests for personal info

🛡️ Protection Measures

  • Verify directly with official websites
  • Never share passwords or OTPs
  • Check sender email addresses
  • Research companies before applying
  • Ask questions when unsure

📞 What To Do If Scammed

  • Stop all contact immediately
  • Report to authorities
  • Change all passwords
  • Monitor financial accounts
  • Report to the platform

🔍 Verification Steps

  • Visit official company website
  • Call verified phone numbers
  • Check domain age & SSL cert
  • Look for contact info
  • Search for company reviews
